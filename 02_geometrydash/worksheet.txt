Minecraft:
1. To mine materials and craft items to defeat the Ender Dragon.
2. WASD Movement, Left Click to Mine, Right Click to Place / Interact

Geometry Dash:

What are the objects? Main Character, Spikes, Platforms
What are the player's actions? Jump
What is the goal? Make it to the end of the level
What causes game over? Hitting the Spikes
How is progress shown? Percentage of level completed
